## Tightening Language

If the model drifts, append one or more of these clauses:

- `flat two-color silhouette only`
- `cut-paper graphic, not illustration rendering`
- `no texture, no painterly surface`
- `no realistic face or anatomy`
- `large simple shapes, very few details`
